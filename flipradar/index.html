FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before signing up.

How does FlipRadar actually find deals? ↓
FlipRadar uses the official eBay API to scan thousands of active listings every 5 minutes. It compares each listing price against the average sold price for that item over the last 90 days. When it finds something listed 30%+ below market value, it emails you instantly with the link and full profit breakdown.
How many alerts will I get per week? ↓
It varies depending on the market — typically between 3-10 alerts per week. We only alert you on genuine opportunities above 25% profit margin, so you won't be flooded with low-quality deals. Quality over quantity.
Do I need any experience to use this? ↓
No experience needed at all. FlipRadar does all the hard work — finding the deal, calculating the profit, and linking you directly to the listing. You just decide whether to buy it and relist it. Most of our users had never sold on eBay before they joined.
What categories does it scan? ↓
FlipRadar currently scans 19 categories including iPhones, Samsung phones, PlayStation consoles, Nintendo Switch, AirPods, MacBooks, iPads, Nike trainers, Adidas Yeezys, Dyson products, cameras, drones and more. We add new categories regularly.
How quickly do I need to act on an alert? ↓
Fast — ideally within 15-30 minutes. Underpriced items get snapped up quickly on eBay. We recommend turning on email notifications on your phone so you see alerts as soon as they arrive, even if you're not at your computer.
Can I cancel anytime? ↓
Yes, completely. Cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal with one click. No questions asked, no cancellation fees. If you cancel during your 7-day free trial you won't be charged a penny.
Is this legal? ↓
100% yes. Buying items and reselling them for profit is completely legal in the UK. FlipRadar simply alerts you to publicly listed items on eBay — the same listings anyone can see. There's nothing against eBay's terms of service about buying and reselling items.
What if I don't make my money back? ↓
FlipRadar costs £9.99/month. A single successful flip — even a small one — covers your subscription cost many times over. That said, results depend on how quickly you act on alerts and market conditions. The 7-day trial lets you see real deals before committing.
