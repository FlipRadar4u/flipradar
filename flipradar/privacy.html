Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026

FlipRadar is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

1. Data We Collect

When you sign up for FlipRadar, we collect:

  • Name and email address — to send you deal alerts and account information
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details
  • Usage data — basic information about how you use the service

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Send you eBay deal alerts by email
  • Manage your subscription
  • Send important service updates
  • Improve FlipRadar

We will never sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Email Communications

By subscribing to FlipRadar, you consent to receiving deal alert emails. These are core to the service and cannot be opted out of while subscribed. You may cancel your subscription at any time to stop receiving emails.

4. Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — for payment processing. Their privacy policy applies to payment data
  • Netlify — for website hosting
  • Zoho Mail — for email delivery
  • eBay API — for sourcing listing data (public data only)

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your subscription is active, and for up to 12 months after cancellation for legal and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@flipradar.co.uk

7. Cookies

FlipRadar's website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@flipradar.co.uk
